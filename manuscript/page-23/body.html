“What was that you were singing, my good woman?”

The woman was ashamed to let him hear what her daughter had been doing, so she sang, instead of that:

“My darter ha' spun five, five skeins to-day. My darter ha' spun five, five skeins to-day.” “Stars o' mine!” said the king, “I never heard tell of any one that could do that.”
